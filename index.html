About

Hi! I'm Billy, a FULL-STACK web developer from South Florida. My day starts with coffee, followed by diving into creating amazing things for the ever-evolving web.

I'm often called a perfectionist, which is why I'm drawn to front-end development and design. It allows me to tap into my creativity while constantly learning new ways to improve my technical skills.

I graduated from 4Geeks Academy in Miami where I gained a wide range of skills and collaborated with my team to build full-stack web applications, giving me hands-on experience in real-world projects.
